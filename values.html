values

value 1

generosity

Sacrificing for community and giving as a culture. I have time and money to give. As a family, we do our best to give at least 10% of our income and we are learning what it looks like to donate our time.

value 2

honesty

Being as authentic as I can to build relationships with people I collaborate with. The more that I can share what I am thinking and my intent, I believe the better working relationships I can have with people.

value 3

trust

Through generosity and honesty, I hope to create trust with the people I partner and work with. Without trust, I don't believe a team can move forward and make the impact they are hoping to make.
